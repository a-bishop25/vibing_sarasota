Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy at Vibing Sarasota

Last Updated: January 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Vibing Sarasota ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Physical addresses or billing information
  • Social security numbers or other government IDs
  • Financial information or payment details

2.2 Information We May Collect

We may collect limited, non-personal information to improve our service:

  • Usage Analytics: Page views, time spent on site, popular content (via Google Analytics)
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, general location (city/state level)
  • Cookies: Small files to remember your preferences and improve site functionality

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Understand which content is most helpful to visitors
  • Ensure our website functions properly across different devices
  • Analyze general usage patterns to enhance our service

4. Cookies and Tracking

4.1 Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies that are necessary for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.

4.2 Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. These cookies help us:

  • Count the number of visitors
  • See which pages are most popular
  • Understand how people navigate our site

4.3 Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookie preferences through our consent banner or your browser settings. You may:

  • Accept all cookies for the best experience
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website usage. Google Analytics may collect information about your use of our website. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

5.2 External Links

Our website contains links to local businesses and external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information. However, since we don't collect personal information, your privacy risk is minimal.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there's no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear your browser cookies at any time
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our About page.

11. GDPR Compliance

For visitors from the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • We obtain consent before using analytics cookies
  • You can withdraw consent at any time
  • We process minimal data and only for legitimate purposes
  • We don't transfer personal data outside the EU
